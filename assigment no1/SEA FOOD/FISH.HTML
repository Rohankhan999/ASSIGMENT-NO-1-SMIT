DESCRIPTION

Fish is a highly nutritious and versatile food enjoyed worldwide. It is rich in essential nutrients such as omega-3 fatty acids, protein, vitamins (such as vitamin D and B12), and minerals (like iodine, iron, and zinc). Fish comes in numerous varieties, each with its own flavor and texture, ranging from mild and flaky to robust and meaty. It can be prepared in various ways including grilling, baking, frying, steaming, and raw (as in sushi and sashimi). Fish dishes are a staple in many cultures, offering a healthy and delicious option for meals.
