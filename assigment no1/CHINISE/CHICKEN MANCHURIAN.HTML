Chicken Manchurian is a popular Indo-Chinese dish widely enjoyed in Pakistan. It features succulent pieces of fried chicken tossed in a tangy and slightly spicy sauce made from a blend of soy sauce, ketchup, and chili sauce. The dish often includes vegetables like bell peppers, onions, and spring onions, adding color and crunch. Chicken Manchurian is typically served with fried rice or noodles, making it a flavorful and satisfying meal. Its vibrant taste and combination of textures make it a favorite in many households and restaurants.
