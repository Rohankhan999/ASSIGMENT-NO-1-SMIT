DESCRIPTION

Chicken Peshawari Karahi is a traditional Pakistani dish known for its rich, aromatic flavors and quick cooking time. Originating from the Peshawar region, this dish features tender chicken pieces cooked in a wok (karahi) with tomatoes, green chilies, garlic, and ginger. It's seasoned with a blend of spices, including cumin, coriander, and garam masala, which gives it a distinctive taste. Fresh cilantro and julienned ginger are often added as a garnish, enhancing its vibrant presentation. Chicken Peshawari Karahi is typically enjoyed with naan or roti, making it a popular and comforting meal in Pakistani cuisine.
