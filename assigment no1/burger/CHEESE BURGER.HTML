DESCRIPTION

A cheeseburger is a classic variation of the traditional hamburger, distinguished by the addition of melted cheese on top of the beef patty. The cheese, commonly cheddar, Swiss, or American, adds a creamy texture and rich flavor that complements the savory beef. Typically, a cheeseburger is served with standard toppings like lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles, along with condiments such as ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise. The combination of juicy beef, melted cheese, and fresh toppings makes the cheeseburger a beloved and iconic dish in American cuisine, enjoyed at diners, fast-food restaurants, and backyard barbecues alike.
