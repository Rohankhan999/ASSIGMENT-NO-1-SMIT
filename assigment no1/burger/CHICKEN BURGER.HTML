DESCRIPTION

A Chicken Burger is a popular alternative to the classic beef burger, featuring a juicy chicken patty made from either grilled, fried, or breaded chicken breast. It's typically served in a bun and topped with fresh ingredients like lettuce, tomato, and mayonnaise, although variations might include cheese, pickles, onions, and various sauces. The chicken burger is known for its lighter, leaner profile compared to beef, making it a versatile option for those seeking a different flavor or a slightly healthier choice without sacrificing taste.
