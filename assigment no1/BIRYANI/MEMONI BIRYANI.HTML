DESCRIPTION

Unique to the Kutch district of Gujarat, the Memon biryani is a beautiful conglomeration of the flavours of Gujarat and Sindh. Breathe in a bit of Kutch's favourite biryani! Ingredients - Red Chilli, Dhania, Haldi, Cumin, Star-anaise, Bayleaf, Black Salt, Cinnamon, Cardamon, Shah Zeera, Black Cardamon & Pepper.
