DESCRIPTION

Chicken Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern street food loved for its flavorful and tender chicken. The dish consists of thinly sliced marinated chicken that's typically grilled on a rotating spit. The chicken is seasoned with a blend of aromatic spices such as cumin, paprika, garlic, and coriander, giving it a distinctive savory taste. It's served wrapped in a warm pita bread or flatbread, accompanied by fresh vegetables like tomatoes, cucumbers, and lettuce. Condiments such as tahini sauce or garlic sauce are often added to enhance the flavor. Chicken Shawarma is known for its delicious combination of juicy chicken, crisp vegetables, and creamy sauces, making it a satisfying and popular choice worldwide.
